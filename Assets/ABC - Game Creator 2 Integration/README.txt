---------------------- DEMO INSTRUCTIONS ----------------------

'GC 2 Basic Integration' is for users who do not yet own GC 2 Inventory and Stats and shows basic combat integration with ABC and GC 2

'GC 2 Full Integration' is for users who have downloaded GC 2, Inventory & Stats and provides a full experience of the capabilities between ABC and GC 2. 

*** Before unzipping the full integration please make sure that ABC, GC 2, Inventory, Stats and ALL GC examples have been downloaded and installed ****

*** Please make sure that you only unzip one integration at a time, either basic or full, as duplicate files exist in both Zips ****
